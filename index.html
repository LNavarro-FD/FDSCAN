How to use
1
Load the guest list — go to ⬆ Upload and import the CSV from Remenham Farm (or your ticketing provider)
2
Open on each device — scan the QR code above or share the URL. Data stays on each device (offline capable)
3
Scan tickets — point camera at QR/barcode. Green flash + vibration = admitted. Red = problem.
4
Manual lookup — use the Guests tab to find by name. Tap individual ticket to check in one person, or use the big button to admit the whole party at once
5
Evening tickets — admitted from 5:00 PM only. Scanner will flag if too early.

Battery optimisation tips
  • Enable Battery saver mode above — reduces camera polling rate
  • Use Guest list tab for manual check-ins instead of keeping camera active
  • Reduce screen brightness to minimum comfortable level
  • Turn off torch when not needed
  • Keep phone plugged in at gate stations where possible
  • Close other apps running in background
