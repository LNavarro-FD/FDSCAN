Door rules
🌙
Evening tickets — valid from 5:00 PM only. Scanner flags if too early — ask guest to return later.
⭐
VIP guests — scan result shows VIP. Issue gold wristband + food tokens.
🎫
Ticketmaster guests — app flags these. Ask to see their Ticketmaster email, then admit manually in Guests tab.
👥
Manual lookup — Guests tab → search by name → tap name to expand → check in one ticket or all at once.
↩
Accidental check-in — expand the booking and tap ↩ Undo on the ticket.
📵
No signal — app works offline once loaded. Check-ins sync when connection returns.
🔋
Battery — use Guests tab for manual check-ins (no camera drain). Keep plugged in at gate where possible.
